What is this

I wrote random tech demos here.
Visit github.com/ch3coohlink/editor and pick a demo that interests you, run it in your browser using:
ch3coohlink.github.io/editor/index.html?load=[target_path]
For example: ch3coohlink.github.io/editor/index.html?load=gui-test/01 - 1 level octree.js .
